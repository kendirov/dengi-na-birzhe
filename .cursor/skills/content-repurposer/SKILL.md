# Content Repurposer

## Трансформации

| From | To | Template |
|------|-----|----------|
| Market brief | Telegram post | `references/telegram-post-template.md` |
| Lesson draft | Slide blocks | `presentation-diagram-generator` skill |
| Transcript | Lesson outline | `trading-lesson-builder` skill |
| Market idea | Screener example | ticker + why in inspector style |
| Complex topic | 3-slide mini-deck | `slide-blocks.md` |

## Telegram style

- Hook первой строкой
- 5–8 коротких абзацев
- Emoji минимум (0–2)
- CTA: курс или скринер
- Disclaimer в конце (мелко)
